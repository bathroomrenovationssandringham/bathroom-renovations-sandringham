A standard bathroom renovation in Sandringham typically takes 4 to 6 weeks. More extensive projects involving structural changes may extend to 8 weeks or longer. The timeline depends on factors such as bathroom size, design complexity, and material availability.

Minor bathroom renovations in Sandringham generally don’t require council approval.
